Mortgage Mate — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Mortgage Mate (iOS app) — a data-driven home loan simulator for Australian buyers. No data is collected, no account required, no in-app purchases.

This privacy policy applies exclusively to the Mortgage Mate iOS app (developed by Zhanglong Cao). It does not apply to any other app or service on this website.

Last updated: 6 March 2026

In One Sentence

Mortgage Mate collects no data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Mortgage Mate is a free, offline home loan simulator. It has no account system, no server backend, no in-app purchases, and no advertising. All calculations are performed locally on your iPhone or iPad using the numbers you enter. Nothing is sent anywhere.

Data Collection

We collect nothing. Specifically:

Category Status
Personal information (name, email, etc.) ✗ Not collected
Financial inputs (income, loan amount, rate, etc.) ✗ Stays on-device only
Location data ✗ Not collected
Device identifiers (IDFA, etc.) ✗ Not collected
Analytics or usage data ✗ Not collected
Crash reports ✗ Not collected
Advertising / tracking ✗ None
Purchase history ✗ No in-app purchases exist

No In-App Purchases

Mortgage Mate is completely free with no subscription tiers, no premium unlock, and no in-app purchases of any kind. There is nothing to buy, and no payment information is ever handled by the app.

On-Device Storage

The app uses iOS UserDefaults solely to:

  • Remember whether you have agreed to the in-app disclaimer
  • Cache publicly available rate/tax parameters (fetched from a config file, e.g. ATO thresholds, APRA buffer)

This data is stored locally and never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Mortgage Mate uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporters. There are no third-party frameworks embedded in the app that could collect data independently.

Data Sharing

We share no data with any third party — because no data is collected in the first place.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If privacy practices change in a future version, this page will be updated with a new “Last updated” date. Significant changes will also be noted in the App Store release notes.
