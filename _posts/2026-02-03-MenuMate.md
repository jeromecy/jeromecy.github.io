## How It Works

1. **Scan**: Take a photo, upload an image, or type menu items
2. **Customize**: Select your dietary goals and group size
3. **Analyze**: AI processes the menu and your preferences
4. **Choose**: Get personalized recommendations with complete nutrition info
5. **Track**: Save to your daily log (Premium feature)

## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 3, 2026**

### Your Privacy Matters
MenuMate is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device.

### ✅ Local Data Storage
Your profile, dietary preferences, allergies, and nutrition tracking data are stored locally on your device using secure storage. We do not collect, store, or have access to this information.

### 🤖 AI Menu Analysis
When you scan a menu, the image is processed by AI services (OpenAI GPT-4, Google Gemini, or DeepSeek) to provide nutrition analysis. Menu images are not stored after analysis. No personal information is sent with menu images.

### 💳 Subscription & Payments
Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not process or store payment information. All transactions go through Apple's secure payment system.

### ❤️ HealthKit Integration
If you enable HealthKit sync, nutrition data will be shared with Apple Health. You control these permissions in iOS Settings. This data is not sent to our servers.

### 🚫 No Analytics or Tracking
MenuMate does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage, location, or behavior.

### 🔐 Your Data, Your Control
You can export your nutrition data at any time. Use 'Reset All Settings' to delete all local data from your device.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How accurate is the nutrition information?**  
A: MenuMate uses advanced AI models to estimate nutrition based on typical serving sizes and ingredients. Estimates are generally accurate but may vary from actual values.

**Q: Can I use MenuMate offline?**  
A: You need an internet connection to analyze menus. However, previously saved recommendations can be viewed offline.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > MenuMate.

**Q: Is my data shared with third parties?**  
A: No. Your personal data stays on your device. Only menu images are sent to AI services for analysis, with no personal information attached.

**Q: What languages are supported?**  
A: MenuMate supports English, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, German, and Italian for recommendations.

**Q: Can I export my nutrition data?**  
A: Yes, Premium users can export their nutrition tracking data. Free users can take screenshots of their recommendations.
