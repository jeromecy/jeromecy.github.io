## What does it do?

Simple: it uses AI to write social media posts for you. You just tell it what you want to post about, pick a style (professional, funny, casual, etc.), and boom – it generates content ready to share.

## Why I made this

Honestly, I got tired of staring at a blank screen trying to think of what to post. Between teaching, research, and everything else, who has time to craft the perfect tweet or LinkedIn update? So I built this app to make it easier.

## The cool parts

- **Different styles**: Need to sound professional on LinkedIn? Done. Want something funny for Twitter? Got it.
- **Multiple platforms**: Works for Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook – you name it.
- **Super quick**: Takes like 30 seconds to generate a post.
- **Multiple options**: Don't like the first version? Generate more until you find one you like.

## Who's it for?

Anyone who posts on social media, really. I built it thinking about fellow academics who need to share their research in plain language, but it's useful for anyone – entrepreneurs, content creators, or just people who want to up their social media game without spending all day on it.
