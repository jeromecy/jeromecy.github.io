## What is MenuMate?

MenuMate is an AI-powered dining companion that helps you make smarter, healthier meal choices at any restaurant. Simply snap a photo of a menu, and MenuMate's AI will analyse the dishes and deliver personalised recommendations based on your dietary goals, food allergies, and preferences — in seconds.

Whether you're eating out with family, managing a health condition, or just curious about what's actually in your meal, MenuMate takes the guesswork out of dining.

---

## Key Features

### 🎯 Smart Menu Analysis
Snap a photo of any menu — printed, digital, or handwritten — and get instant AI-powered recommendations tailored to you. Supports **8 languages**: English, Chinese, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, German, and Italian.

### 🥗 Personalised to Your Goals
Set your dietary goals — Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, Heart Healthy, Low Carb, High Protein, or Vegetarian — and let MenuMate surface the best dishes for you. Allergy and restriction management built-in.

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Group Dining Mode
Dining with family or friends? Group mode gives recommendations that work for everyone at the table — adults, kids, and seniors.

### 📊 Nutrition Insights
Detailed breakdown of calories, protein, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals for recommended dishes.

### 💪 Extra Features
- Daily nutrition tracking with trends and analytics
- **Apple HealthKit integration** — sync your meals directly to Apple Health
- Priority support

## Privacy First

MenuMate was built with privacy at the core:
- **All profile data stays on your device** — we never see it
- AI analysis is done with your **explicit consent only**
- **No analytics, no ad tracking, no data selling**
- Subscriptions handled entirely through Apple's secure system

When AI analysis is used, menu photos and preferences are sent to OpenAI (GPT-4), Google Gemini, or DeepSeek (for mainland China users) in real-time and are not permanently stored after analysis completes.
